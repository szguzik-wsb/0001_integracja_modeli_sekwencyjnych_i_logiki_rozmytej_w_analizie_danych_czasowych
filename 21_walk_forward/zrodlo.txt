# Autor: mgr inz. Szymon Guzik, Uniwersytet WSB Merito w Gdansku

Eksperyment 21: Walk-forward validation

Metoda badawcza:
  Walk-forward validation (expanding/sliding window cross-validation for time series)

Zrodla metodologiczne:
  - Tashman, L. J. (2000). Out-of-sample tests of forecasting accuracy: an analysis and review.
    International Journal of Forecasting, 16(4), 437-450.
    DOI: 10.1016/S0169-2070(00)00065-0
  - Bergmeir, C., & Benitez, J. M. (2012). On the use of cross-validation for time series
    predictor evaluation. Information Sciences, 191, 192-213.
    DOI: 10.1016/j.ins.2011.12.028

Dane:
  - S&P 500, WIG20, EUR/USD, BTC/USD (z badania/zewnetrzne/)
  - Yahoo Finance (yfinance) oraz Stooq.pl

Implementacja:
  - Python 3.14, PyTorch (TCN), scikit-fuzzy (Mamdani), statsmodels (ARIMA)
  - 5 rund walk-forward, 60/10/30 split, przesuniecie 10%
  - Seed: 42

Reprodukowalnosc:
  - RANDOM_SEED = 42
  - Parametry identyczne z eksperymentem 05 (TCN-Mamdani hybrid)
